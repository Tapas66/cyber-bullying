Cyberbullying Facts & Statistics

Cyberbullying affects millions of teens and adults worldwide. Stay informed with the latest data.

  • 1 in 3 young people has experienced cyberbullying.
  • 70% of teens have seen online bullying.
  • 37% of young people between 12-17 have been bullied online.

Prevention Tips

Think Before You Post

Be mindful of what you share online. Don’t share personal information that can be misused.

Block and Report

If you are being harassed, block the person and report them to the platform.

Educate Yourself

Understand your rights and the policies of the platforms you use.

Encourage Open Communication

Talk to friends, family, or a counselor if you face online bullying.

Raise Awareness

Speak out against cyberbullying and encourage others to do the same.

Protect Your Privacy

Adjust your privacy settings to control who can see your information.
